Earnest Education

Earnest Education is headquartered in Vancouver, Canada, and was founded by senior teaching theory researchers and a team of front-line teachers. Since its establishment, Earnest Education has been focusing on the exchange and cooperation between China and Canada in culture and education, providing students and parents with one-stop study abroad, education and training services. Tailor-made talent programs, while sending students to world-renowned institutions, train students into active learners with lifelong learning hobbies.
