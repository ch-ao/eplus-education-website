Introduction

孩子学习速算的目的不单是为了计算，更重要的是开发孩子的智力，增强自信心！ 儿童学习新加坡数学的四大优点： 1. 促进其左右脑平衡发展； 2. 培养孩子的协调能力； 3. 培养孩子的专注精神； 4. 使儿童的计算能力迅速提高。

新加坡数学与BC省数学大纲对比起来，计算，几何，数据管理，统计等样样不缺。在北美有很多私立学校都选用它为数学教材，新加坡数学在级别上比相对应BC省数学稍难，BC省数学的内容详尽包含，另外加入了更多的计算题，应用题，及心算课程和练习。所以课外选择新加坡数学对于数学成绩提高潜移默化，事半功倍。

知行教育的新加坡数学在教育方法上，注重掌握，通过有意的概念排序并且在教学内容上选取Olympiad教材，去除了传统数学课的boring和simple，同时深入浅出地数学课堂形式使得学生们收获满满且欢乐多多。

该方法的一些关键特征包括CPA(具体的、图形的、抽象的)级数、数字键、条形模型和心算。学生们不再是死记硬背，而是学习数学思维，并依赖于之前课程中获得的知识深度。

Concrete, Pictorial, Abstract (CPA) Approach

The Concrete, Pictorial, Abstract (CPA) approach develops a deep understanding of math through building on existing knowledge.

In the concrete phase, students interact with physical objects to model problems.

In the pictorial phase, they make a mental connection between the objects they just handled and visual representations of those objects.

In the abstract phase, students use symbolic modeling of problems using numbers and math symbols (+, −, ×, ÷).

具体、图形、抽象(CPA)方法通过建立在现有知识上发展对数学的深刻理解。

在具体阶段，学生与物理对象互动来建模问题。

在图形阶段，他们在刚刚处理过的物体和这些物体的视觉表征之间建立了一种心理联系。

在抽象阶段，学生使用数字和数学符号(+，−，×，÷)对问题进行符号建模。

Example

This problem from Singapore Math L1 shows the stages of the CPA approach, incorporating the use of number bonds to illustrate addition.

5 birds are on a branch. 2 more birds come. How many birds will be on the branch altogether?

Concrete Stage

In this stage, teachers lead a classroom activity.

Students represent birds. Have 5 students go to the front of the class and ask the rest of the class how many students there are. Send 2 more students up to the front and ask, “How many students are there now?” Ask students to explain what happened. The interaction introduces students to the problem in a tangible way. 

Pictorial Stage

Students are then shown a visual representation of the problem.

Bar Modeling

Bar models are a versatile and transferable tool that students can use to visualize a range of math concepts, such as fractions, ratios, percentages, and more. Drawing bar models for word problems allows students to determine the knowns and unknowns in a given situation. It extends the CPA approach, especially the pictorial phase, as it allows students to illustrate the mathematical information given in problems. It prepares them to understand more complex math on a conceptual level. This method is most effective when used frequently throughout the program.

Mental Math

The Singapore Math approach teaches techniques and skills to easily and accurately perform mental math. These strategies help students develop number sense and flexibility in thinking about numbers. Many mental math strategies involve factoring numbers into parts, then performing operations on them in a different order from the original expression. The thought processes involved in mental math are often illustrated by number bonds.

认为数学是重要的和可接近的态度也是必不可少的。当学生的理解和成功的潜力被假定时，他们的表现在更高的水平。

那么，这与广泛传统教授数学的方式有什么不同呢?

在典型的传统数学程序中，学生先得到一个现成的例子，然后根据这个例子来解决问题，用不同的数字重复同样的步骤。在新加坡数学中，学生必须从一开始就对概念进行思考，并以新的方式加以应用。由于他们不能依赖简单的复制，学生们被推向了更大的参与度和更广阔的思维。在传统的数学课程中，概念和技能在不同年级之间和不同年级之间被划分得更细，而新加坡的数学课程则贯穿着与过去学习的紧密联系。

新加坡数学不仅帮助学生更成功地解决问题，它还帮助他们获得自信和足智多谋的感觉，因为它坚持概念的深度。这自然会让学生在更高级的数学中出类拔萃。
